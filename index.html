ZigTech was founded by a U.S. Navy submarine veteran with over two decades of enterprise network engineering experience across both federal and commercial sectors. Our principal brings deep technical expertise from senior roles at Cisco and multiple network technology companies serving global enterprise and government clients.

We specialize in network observability, AIOps, and IT infrastructure — a high-demand capability set for both federal agencies managing large, multi-cloud network environments and commercial enterprises undergoing digital transformation.

Based in Cocoa, Florida, ZigTech is an SBA-certified HUBZone, SDVOSB, and VOSB firm committed to delivering mission-quality outcomes with the discipline and accountability that comes from military service.
